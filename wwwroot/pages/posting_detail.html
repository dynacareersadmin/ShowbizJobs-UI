Executive Assistant

HBO

New York, NY 02-29-2020
  • Details
  • Apply
  • Paid
  • Full Time

Job Description

Administrative support (schedule meetings, manage calendars, travel arrangements, expense reports, handle phones, mailings, etc.) for the Senior Vice President of Corporate Affairs and Corporate Social Responsibility for Warner Media Inc.

PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:
• Administrative support (schedule meetings, manage calendars, travel arrangements, expense reports, handle phones, mailings, etc.) for the Senior Vice President of Corporate Affairs and Corporate Social Responsibility
• Coordinate internal CSR event logistics including working with Design + Production to create advertisements and invitations, ordering catering, organizing guest list, working check-in and monitoring the venue (3-5 events/month)
• Research potential nonprofit and partner organization for CSR initiatives
• Provide data entry support for GIFTS (philanthropic giving software) and coordinate logistics for events, galas and general sponsorships with organizations
• Assist in ad hoc assignments as needed including but not limited to writing/editing festival materials, activation letters, presentations decks, press releases and remarks.
• Track CSR and Corporate Affairs employee attendance logs and vacation schedules

REQUIREMENTS:
• Minimum 3 years related experience, college degree preferred
• Social impact/social responsibility experience preferred
• Ability to work in a fast paced environment with minimal supervision
• Candidate must have strong organizational, interpersonal & communication skills (oral & written)
• Must be a team player who is flexible and can multi-task. Must have strong attention to detail
• Must be able to work over-time as needed, assist with evening events.
• Proficient in MS Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint
