Position Title is required. It should be as specific as possible, ideally what would be seen on the person's business card. (Ex: Assistant to HR Director).
  • Do not use ALL CAPS or your posting will be rejected.
  • Do not put extraneous info like ** IMMEDIATE HIRE ** or ASAP!!!
  • Internships should all be prefaced with "Intern - "
  • Avoid using lingo terms that don't convey meaning to everyone (ex. terms like 'Guru' or 'Analyst II')
  • Only sales positions can be classified as Commission
  • We do not accept postings for unpaid modeling, unpaid writing, talent scouts, unlicensed talent agents, personal representation
A location is required to match your listing with candidates near your area. Postal/Zip code is especially important as cities like Los Angeles can be large enough to make travel an issue depending on where the candidate lives.

If this is a remote work opportunity, just use the location of your company's offices.
